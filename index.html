Popular courses offered

Bachelor of Information Technology and Systems (BITS)

It is aimed at training students with sound skills in computer networking, application development and computer-based software keeping in mind the latest trends in IT sector. This course makes sure that students gain deep knowledge in fundamentals and concepts of information technology. Students are provided with proper training and experience during this coures to complete challenging industry requirements.

Master of Information Technology And Systems (MITS)

This course provides in-depth experience and knowledge to the aspirants by following system-wide approach. This expands potential employement opportunities and makes students equipped with the tools that can be used to solve crtical IT problems.

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

We have included latest management concepts and industry information into our curriculum. Four specialization streams are provided in this course:
Stream 1: Management
Stream 2: Information Systems
Stream 3: Finance and Accounting
Stream 4: Tourism and Hospitality
